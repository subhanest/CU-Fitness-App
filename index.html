Some common workouts for maintaining physical fitness:

Physical Exercise is one of the most important factors of a fitness Plan. Here you will find some common workouts with video tutorial to guide you.

  • Cardio Workouts(Improve heart health & endurance)
    1. Running or Jogging – Great for cardiovascular fitness and leg strength.

    2. Cycling – Strengthens legs and improves endurance.

    3. Jump Rope – Excellent for full-body coordination and cardio.

    4. Swimming – Low-impact, full-body workout that builds endurance.

    5. High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) – Short bursts of intense exercise followed by rest.
